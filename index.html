about me

I am a recent graduate from Juno College's web development Bootcamp. I consider myself a lifelong learner. In an industry that is always evolving and constantly changing, I will always have the opportunity to interact with new tools and learn new skills as I begin my new career in web development.


Prior to attending Juno, I was in hospitality where my values of helping others and creating and maintaining relationships helped me work my way from a server to a manager. I am someone who adds value to a team using my strong interpersonal and communication skills provide a positive experience for both my team as well as clients.


When I’m not coding, I love spending time cooking and baking. I equally enjoy watching people share their recipes on Youtube. Most of my recipes, if not all by now, are inspired by those wonderful creators. I am also an escape room enthusiasts! I was hooked after my first experience, something about discovering clues, and solving a series of riddles and puzzles all within a set amount of time motivates and excites me.
